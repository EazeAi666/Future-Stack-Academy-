FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need prior design experience? +
No. The course starts from the very beginning with design fundamentals. Total beginners will follow just as well as those with some experience who want to level up with AI tools.
What software do I need? +
You'll need access to Figma (free tier works), Canva (free tier works), and ideally an Adobe Creative Cloud subscription for the Firefly modules. Midjourney requires a paid plan (from $10/mo). We point you to free alternatives wherever possible.
How long does the course take? +
Most students complete the full course in 8–12 weeks studying 5–7 hours per week. You have lifetime access, so you can go faster or slower based on your schedule.
Is the AI tools section updated regularly? +
Yes. The AI landscape moves fast, and we update the AI modules quarterly to reflect new features and newly important tools. All enrolled students get these updates for free.
Will this help me get freelance clients or a design job? +
Module 6 is entirely dedicated to career and freelance strategy. You'll leave with a polished portfolio, a pricing framework, and a practical plan for landing your first client or design role.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes — we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you're not satisfied after completing the first two modules, we'll refund you in full.
